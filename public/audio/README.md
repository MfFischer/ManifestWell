## Recommended Sources (Royalty-Free)

### Free Sources
1. **Pixabay Music** - https://pixabay.com/music/
   - Completely free for commercial use
   - No attribution required
   - High quality meditation/ambient tracks

2. **Freesound** - https://freesound.org/
   - Creative Commons licensed
   - Great for nature sounds
   - Check individual licenses

3. **Free Music Archive** - https://freemusicarchive.org/
   - Various CC licenses
   - Good ambient music

### Paid Sources (Better Quality)
1. **Artlist** - https://artlist.io/ (~$200/year)
   - Full commercial license
   - Excellent quality

2. **Epidemic Sound** - https://epidemicsound.com/
   - Subscription model
   - Great meditation tracks

### AI-Generated Audio
1. **ElevenLabs** - For guided meditation voice
2. **Mubert** - AI-generated meditation music
3. **AIVA** - AI music composition

## Audio Specifications

For best compatibility:
- Format: MP3
- Bitrate: 128-256 kbps
- Sample rate: 44.1 kHz
- Channels: Stereo (or mono for voice)

## How to Add New Tracks

1. Download/create the audio file
2. Place in appropriate subdirectory
3. Update `src/lib/audio/tracks.ts` with track metadata
4. Include proper attribution if required
